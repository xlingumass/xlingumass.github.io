Notes on IRB

Plan well, plan ahead! Your protocol must be submitted, reviewed and approved in writing by the IRB via UMass e-Protocol before you can start a study. IRB approval can take 4-6 weeks and is valid for a maximum period of one year (unless the study was found to be exempt, in which case it’s three years). Important deadlines Applications not requiring full board review are reviewed on a rolling basis upon submission of all necessary materials....
